New 7 Wonders of the World

Chichén Itzá

Ancient Mayan city in Mexico, known for its pyramid El Castillo.

Christ the Redeemer

Iconic statue in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, overlooking the city.

Colosseum

Roman amphitheater in Italy, symbol of ancient Roman engineering.

Machu Picchu

Incan citadel in the Andes Mountains of Peru.

Petra

Ancient city in Jordan, famous for its rock-cut architecture.

Taj Mahal

Mausoleum in India, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan.

Great Wall of China

Series of fortifications across northern China, stretching over 21,000 km.
